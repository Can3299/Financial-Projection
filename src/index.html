i How is it calculated?

Compound Interest

Interest is calculated on the initial principal and the accumulated interest of previous periods.

Result = P * (1 + r)^t
Simple Interest

Interest is calculated solely on the principal amount. The growth is constant every duration.

Result = P * (1 + (r * t))
  • • P: Principal (Initial money)
  • • r: Interest rate (As a decimal)
  • • t: Time (Number of periods)
